Make 'Em Laugh

A tribute to Robin Williams

Robin Williams brought joy to millions of people around the world with his comedy.

A timeline of his career:

Robin was credited in at least 106 roles between 1977 and 2014, these are just a few examples.
  • Good Morning, Vietnam (1986) - One of his first serious roles that garnered a ton of praise.
  • Dead Poets Society (1989) - An instant classic
  • Hook (1991) - Well recieved, but not as well recieved as some of the others in this list.
  • Popeye (1980) - One of the first roles he's remembered for.
  • Aladdin (1992) - One of his most famous works, showing off a huge range of vocal talents.
  • Mrs. Doubtfire (1993) - Third Golden Globe Best Actor win
  • Good Will Hunting (1997) - Best Supporting Actor award from the Academy Awards, and a SAG Best Supporting Actor award.
  • One Hour Photo (2002) - One of his best known horror movies
  • Robots (2005) - One of his most underrated childrens movies (in my opinion)
  • Happy Feet (2006) - Critically acclaimed childrens movie, even in a supporting role.
  • World's Greatest Dad (2009) - Dark comedy that started to show some of the deeper emotional struggles Robin was going through.
This is a very short list of some of the accomplishments of a very great man. His comedy and humor brought joy to millions of people and his voice was a part of growing up for generations. He will be remembered and missed. For more information, please visit his wikipedia page.
